Education
  • Master of Environmental Management — University of Queensland, 2023
  • Bachelor of Marine Science — University of Queensland, 2023
  • Laurea Triennale Natural Sciences — University of Bologna, 2020

My first contact with how industry accounts for its environmental obligations was at METServe, quantifying foliar damage from SO2 emissions and feeding results into impact monitoring outputs. At Geoconsult the work sat at the exploration end of the mining cycle, where remote sensing identified coal targets and lithology gave the geological context beneath them, Python models built from lab desorption data quantified gas content and emission , and spatial outputs supported EA approval submissions. Hydrobiology brought the ecological and regulatory side that threatened species habitat mapping under the EPBC Act and AUSRIVAS bioassessment across reference catchments. Queensland Government has been the spatial infrastructure underneath all of it, native title claims feeding into court proceedings, vegetation datasets governing what landholders can legally clear, and place name legislation applied at state scale.

My Academic path ran from natural sciences at Bologna across biological, earth, and environmental disciplines, to marine science and environmental management at Brisbane. Research covered Diamantina Basin carbon sequestration using HYDRUS-2D and Great Barrier Reef carbonate chemistry under ANZG ecotoxicological frameworks.

Multilingual across Chinese, English and Italian. Volunteered since 2015 for GBIF, NGOs and Climate Science topics, translating scientific documentary and reports content into Chinese. Since Australia, volunttered with research vessel for reef health across Moreton Bay and the Whitsundays.
